    Providing a good cause with a better website

    Website design and development for a professional athlete's foundation

    The Tsunami Waves Foundation was founded by star Cardinal’s pitcher, Carlos Martinez in 2015. The Tsunami Waves Foundation is an organization that helps children both here in St. Louis, and in his home country of the Dominican Republic, reach their fullest potential and enjoy life.

    We, here at DELT, are huge fans of three things: St. Louis, the Cardinals, and sports. Having the opportunity to work with an organization that dealt with all three things was a dream come true. After Carlos signed a contract extension, him and the foundation decided to increase marketing efforts for Tsunami Waves.

    Their previous website was built using Weebly, and they wanted to enhance usability and give the website’s design a better overall look. We were honored to help a foundation promoting such a good cause and continue to work with them to this day.
