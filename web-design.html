Our web design process

  • UX design & Wireframe designs

    Before we begin, we get an idea of the pages our clients want on their new website, or want to add to their current one. We then create our user-flow chart and wireframe to use as a guide for our web design and development process.

  • You review website designs

    Once we are finished with our initial designs, they are given to our clients for review. We then continue to make changes until they are 100% satisfied with their website's design before we begin development to ensure the quickest possible process.

  • Begin web development

    We typically develop our websites using WordPress, Drupal websites, or HTML, CSS, & JS templates. Once we have the framework built, we start putting the pages together and include all of the on-site SEO services necessary to gain organic search traffic.

  • Launch & monitor website

    Once development is complete and it is approved by you, we publish your new website. We provide the option to run the website on your servers or if we are providing web hosting services, we will launch it on ours. We then continue to monitor the website to make sure it continues to run smoothly once it begins to get more traffic.
