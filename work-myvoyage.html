    Web design and branding for a unique travel website

    During the process of making some posters and print designs for a client, we ended up creating a brand we loved. myvoyage is a travel agency concept that we put together over the course of a few hours. We love the idea of a modern travel agency web design, so we expanded our designs from a few posters to a logo design and website design.

    There are a lot of additional ideas we intend to incorporate, but in just a few hours, we managed to create some awesome landing page designs, a logo, and a few posters. We are currently in the process of designing and developing a fully-functional drag-and-drop trip planner/scheduler. Check back in a few weeks to see it in action!
