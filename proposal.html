Project scope

Based on the RFP document and our intitial research, we have created a list of our key areas of emphasis for your project. Individual details and strategies may change based upon future discussions, but this information will give a clear understanding as to how we will handle your project.

Areas of focus

modern, brand-oriented web design

We will build a modern website that is designed to match your brand and enhance your online appearance. We intend on using your new brand approach by putting a great deal of emphasis on the healthcare industry and innovation.

Increased website traffic

As previously mentioned, one of our most notable distinctions is our expertise in both design and SEO. Our SEO specialists will perform in-depth industry research to determine which keywords should be implemented throughout the website.

Increased conversions

Not only will we help increase the traffic coming to your website, but we will also turn these visitors into leads. We research what conversion tactics work best in your industry and use this information to determine things like contact form placement, recommended form fields, surveys, whitepaper downloads, newsletter sign ups and more.

We know you have a stated different lead list strategies, and we will provide our recommendations after future discussions.

Your portfolio will be used to not only portray your expertise but also for helping build your new identity. To do this we will put an emphasis on projects most relevant to your target market (I.e. healthcare projects) and highlight the inovative approach you took in your work.

Content & topic generation

If you have topics in mind for things like blog articles or whitepapers, we will help generate structured content using our keyword research. We will also help generate new ideas for topics and whitepapers that will be provided by our SEO specialists.

proposed services

Every project is different, and once we begin researching your industry, we almost always generate new ideas and steps to take in your project. Using the RFP and our initial ideas, we have put together our intended workflow and the services we intend to provide.

Services and workflow

ux designing & user-flow mapping

Once our initial research steps have been completed, we will beging creating wireframes for your website. To start, we will create a user-flow map which will help visualize the journies users will take when browsing your website. This will help us determine how we navigate them to where we want them to be (conversion pages) and how we can convert them (fill out a contact form or schedule a meeting).

Once we have the sitemaps and wireframes generated, we can then begin designing the pages. We always start with your website's homepage because this style can then be easily transferred to your interior pages. We offer unlimited design revisions so you are always 100% satisfied with the look of your website before we begin development.

You will recieve access to password-protected software that will allow you to preview your designs in a realistic environment (how they will look in a browser). This software will allow you to comment directly onto the designs so you can give any feedback necessary.

SEO researching and planning

While our designers are working on the website's design, our SEO experts will be researching your industry to determine which keywords should be targeted throughout the website. During this time, they also compile a list of high-volume phrases that can be used for things like blog topics and white papers.

Web development

We will develop a completely custom, user-friendly framework build on the CMS WordPress. We have experience with several content management systems, but we typically recommend WordPress for business/professional websites.

When building a WordPress website, we code everything from scratch and never use pre-built themes. We include a minimal amount of files to ensure fast page speeds. Additionally, we include custom scripts that generate lossless compressed images and minified code assets to maximize page speed and reduce server load time.

Many of the individual elements will be determined upon additional conversations with you, but we will integrate any or all of the following features:

  • Google Analytics setup and integration
  • Calendly, CRM, or other third-party tools integrated into contact forms
  • Conditional contact forms based on user answers
  • Surveys and additional forms
  • Social media feeds
  • SSL certificate installation

    • During this development, you will be able to view a live version of your new website on a password-protected URL. This will make it easy for you to track our progress and make any changes you wish.

    Website launch & hosting

    Once the designs and developed website have been approved we will transfer your website to your primary URL and move it to one of our secure servers. We make sure no emails are lost and your mail records are either switched to our server or edited to remain on their current server.
